Lucy Hale
About

Lucy Hale is an American actress, singer and producer. She has captured the attention of millions through her dynamic on-screen performances in some of the most buzzed about projects in film and television. In 2021 Hale begins shooting the AMC series "Ragdoll." Past television credits include the HBO Max/CW series "Katy Keene" in the titular role and the hugely popular "Pretty Little Liars." Feature film credits include the upcoming "Borrego" and "The Hating Game," both of which she co-produced, and "Big Gold Brick" with Andy Garcia, Oscar Isaac and Megan Fox. Lucy has won multiple awards for her performances including People's Choice Awards, Teen Choice Awards and she was presented with the Gracie Award for Outstanding Performance by a Female Rising Star.
